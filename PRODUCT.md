# Product

## Register

brand

## Product / Job

Sinar Farma is an Indonesian apotek company-profile landing page. Its job is to introduce the brand clearly, show the general kinds of health needs visitors may be looking for, and guide visitors toward an official contact path without inventing contact details or promising transactions.

The page must feel like a concise brand introduction, not a marketplace, hospital, clinic, ecommerce checkout, or medical-advice surface.

## Audience

Visitors in Indonesia who want quick reassurance that Sinar Farma is relevant to everyday apotek needs before deciding how to reach out through official channels.

They may be scanning on mobile, may not want long explanations, and need plain Indonesian copy that is terpercaya, ramah, and cepat.

## Brand Personality

Terpercaya, ramah, cepat.

- **Terpercaya:** structured, careful, factual, and easy to verify.
- **Ramah:** warm enough to feel helpful, but never casual or jokey.
- **Cepat:** concise, direct, and easy to scan.

## Allowed Homepage Content

Allowed homepage sections are limited to:

1. Hero: one Indonesian headline, one short support paragraph, one safe primary contact or anchor CTA, and at most one secondary category anchor.
2. Category snapshot: general categories such as obat bebas, vitamin dan suplemen, alat kesehatan, and kebutuhan kesehatan harian.
3. Brand/value strip: terpercaya, ramah, cepat as experience values only, without metrics or proof claims.
4. Short about block: a brief explanation that Sinar Farma is an apotek profile for general health needs.
5. Contact CTA: safe contact intent only. If no verified channel exists, say that official contact details will be provided through official channels.
6. Optional FAQ only if it remains short, factual, and safe.

## Forbidden Content

Do not add or imply:

- WhatsApp links, phone numbers, email addresses, street addresses, branch locations, or opening hours.
- Licenses, certifications, awards, insurance claims, pharmacist names, registration numbers, testimonials, reviews, ratings, rankings, stats, prices, discounts, product brands, or stock availability.
- Prescription ordering, consultation, diagnosis, medical advice, treatment guidance, emergency service, delivery, pickup, checkout, cart, appointment, or marketplace flows.
- Fake legal, business, social, or contact facts.
- Any claim that Sinar Farma is a clinic, hospital, or care provider beyond a safe apotek profile.
- Template claims, English demo copy, or AstroWind marketing language.

## Concise Indonesian Copy Rules

- Use Indonesian for user-facing copy.
- Keep headings literal and short, ideally 10 words or fewer.
- Keep paragraphs to one idea and no more than two short sentences.
- Prefer everyday visitor language over medical or corporate jargon.
- Avoid hype, urgency tricks, inflated authority, and clever labels that slow scanning.
- Write contact copy as intent, not as a promise of a specific channel.

## No Fake Facts

Every factual claim must be either visible in verified source material or phrased as a non-specific brand intention. If a detail is not provided, omit it or state only that official details are not published yet.

Guardrail language about prohibited facts is allowed in this document because it is non-user-facing planning material.

## Medical / Transactional Safety

The page may mention general apotek categories, but must not guide treatment decisions, imply prescription fulfillment, imply consultation, or promise transactional service. Category copy must describe browsing context only, not availability, advice, or outcomes.

## Light / Dark Expectation

Preserve the existing light and dark theme system. `ui.theme` must remain set to `system` unless a future verified user instruction changes it.

Both themes must be readable, AA-oriented, and tested together. Dark mode is not a separate brand and must not introduce stronger claims, hidden content, or different contact promises.

## Skill Preflight Requirement

Before future homepage, token, navigation, or visual edits, load and follow the relevant project skills: using-superpowers discipline, Astro, Tailwind/design-system guidance, Impeccable or frontend design guidance, and copywriting when changing persuasive copy.

## Safe Contact Strategy

Until verified contact details are supplied, CTAs should point to an on-page contact section or neutral route and use copy such as “Lihat cara menghubungi” or “Menuju informasi kontak.” Do not invent WhatsApp, telephone, email, address, hours, social handles, or map links.

## Definition of Done

The foundation is complete when future executors can update homepage, navigation, design tokens, and route cleanup without inventing strategy, fake facts, unsafe claims, or ad-hoc visual rules.
